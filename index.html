Modern Movies

Movies from 2000 to Today.

Upgrade

Review

The camera work and Logan-Marshall Green's Movements are the highlights in this film. The action feels deadly precise, and it's so cool to watch.

Avatar

Review

Stunning visuals. Wish the story had as much effort put into it as the effects.

Van Helsing

Review

Technically, it's not a great film. But the sheer shameless camp makes it an incredibly enjoyable ride.

Skinamarink

Review

This is a cruel film. It rejects clarity and convention in favor of torturing the viewer with the uncanny. It's not fun. But it's effective.

The One

Review

Goofy film with solid action and a hilarious soundtrack. Ending scene alone is worth the price of admission.

Strange World

Review

I'm biased. I love pulp adventure shlock. This movie feels like a loving send-up, with fun characters and a clever approach to its messaging.

Jupiter Ascending

Review

Listen. It's bad. It's so, so bad. But's also very funny. I want a whole movie just with Eddie Redmayne's character.

Valerian and the City of a Thousand Planets

Review

Imaginative visuals and neat ideas, but I was way more interested in the city than whatever Valerian had going on.

Evil Dead Rise

Review

Delightfully creepy and nasty as hell. This is a film that embraces its source material while still carving out a fresh niche for itself.

Madame Web

Review

There is so much wrong with this film. The editing is all over the place. The story makes no sense. There is an egregious amount of obvious product placement. The only things that made the film even slightly enjoyable were Zooey Deschanel's ironic, checked-out performance and Tahar Rahim's stunningly bad ADR dialogue.

Bullet Train

Review

It's a good time. The action is slick, the characters were fun, and the plot has some neat twists. The celebrity appearances got a little annoying.
